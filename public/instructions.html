NNFX Manual Backtester

The purpose of this app is to facilitate manual checks of tentative indicators to test if they should be further tested in full model or discarded. To use, select a pair to test and a start/end period of time to test. Select a(n) indicator(s) (minimum required is entry indicator and parameters) and press Start test. Now use MT5 to see the selected pair, period of time and chosen indicators and parameters. Go through every trade as defined by NNFX conditions and your chosen setup. input each trade in the trade input area. Once you go through the chosen test period, press the End & submit button. All data will be stored in database and a quick preview result will be shown onscreen. To see full result analysis and past tests, click on results button (once test is finished or there is no data input. Do not leave unfinished tests without erasing them from db.
